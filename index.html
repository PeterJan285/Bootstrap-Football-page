Introducing UEFA Play!

UEFA's new best-in-class audio platform is tapping into fans' love of both football and music, bringing the excitement of matchday to life through unique playlists.

What is it?

UEFA Play is a cross-platform hub housing all things football and music, bringing all UEFA audio properties under one umbrella, and exciting fans with 'football x music' crossovers and collaborations.

Accessible at campaigns.uefa.com/play, the website is the go-to destination for the sounds of football, with the UEFA Champions League, UEFA Women's Champions League and UEFA Europa League anthems living on its playlists.
